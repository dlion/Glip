Glip

Copy and Paste from and to everywhere through internet

Glip lets you move information between computers using unique links

Example: http://{{hostname}}/url/{{URLrandom}}.
Visit the URL and paste in what you want.
In another computer/device visit the same URL to retrieve the information.

For your security the information saved on the URL will be destroyed after you've been read it.
Anyone visiting the same URL at a later time will not be able to see your information (already gone forever).

You can use Glip to move data between devices without using e-mail, move some clipboard datas between two seperate computers.
